Series: An Introduction to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Algorithms

This series is intended for readers who are interested in a technical introduction to the foundational mathematical algorithms used in Artificial Intelliengce and Machine Learning. No prior knowledge or experience in Artificial Intelligence or Machine Learning is required. Although there is a fair amount of math to work through, knowledge of mathematics at a high school level will suffice.
